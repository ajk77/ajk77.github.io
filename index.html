Bio

Andrew J. King is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Critical Care Medicine at the University of Pittsburgh. His academic background is rooted in informatics, beginning with a BS in Bioinformatics, followed by a PhD in Biomedical Informatics, and a postdoctoral fellowship in Critical Care Informatics. Primarily focusing on the people, processes, and technologies of the ICU, Dr. King designs, implements, and evaluates clinical decision support systems that increase evidence adoption and improve care team effectiveness and wellbeing.
