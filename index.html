Bio

Andrew J King is an Assistant Professor of Critical Care Medicine at the University of Pittsburgh School of Medicine. His research centers on applying informatics and artificial intelligence methods to better understand critical illness and improve teamwork in the Intensive Care Unit. He also develops Learning Health System and research infrastructure, such as automated methods for process and outcome measurement.
